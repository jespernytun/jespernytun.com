About

I'm a Norwegian engineering student in my third year at Institut National des Sciences Appliquées de Toulouse, studying automation and electronics. My coursework spans control systems, signal processing, analog and digital electronics, and computer architecture. Currently aiming for a double degree track in finance.

Outside of coursework I tend to work on projects that scratch an itch — whether that's automating something tedious at work, building a graphical application from scratch, or digging into finance out of curiosity. I'm also involved in student life through Tekna, the Norwegian association for technology and science graduates.

I'm aiming for a career at the intersection of engineering and finance, where I can apply technical skills to quantitative and qualitative modelling to help in decision making processes.
